What happens

Exactly what we do
in 14 days.

No black box. Here's every step of the audit process, what we need from you at each stage, and what you get at the end.

1
Application review & intake call
We review your application and schedule a 30-minute call to understand your operation, current systems, and the area you're most concerned about. We'll confirm which data sources to connect and set expectations for what the audit will cover.
Day 1–2 · 30 min of your time
2
System connection & data pull
We connect 1–2 of your core systems via read-only API or secure file transfer. No changes to your systems, no additional software. We pull 6–12 months of operational data depending on availability.
Day 2–4 · 30 min of your time to share credentials
3
Analysis & pattern detection
Our team runs the data through our operational intelligence models. We look for cost leaks, process inefficiencies, supplier anomalies, and automation opportunities. This runs in the background — no further input needed from you.
Day 4–12 · No time required from you
4
Findings report & presentation
We deliver a written report covering: every anomaly found, the estimated cost of each, and a prioritised list of what to fix first. We then walk you through it on a 45-minute call. No PowerPoint fluff — just the data and what it means for your business.
Day 13–14 · 45 min presentation call
5
Your decision — no pressure
If the report shows clear, quantified opportunities, we'll outline what a retainer engagement would look like to automate and fix them. If you'd rather act on it yourself, the report is yours to keep. We don't follow up aggressively — our work does the talking.
After Day 14 · No obligation whatsoever
