Examples

Simple examples of how we can
streamline and revolutionise your business.

Most of the money UK manufacturers lose isn't taken in one go. It leaks — through supplier invoices nobody cross-checks, overtime nobody analyses, and stock nobody monitors until the year-end write-off. Each system below is designed to close one of those leaks.

These are illustrative examples of the systems we build. Each one targets a real, common problem in manufacturing and logistics — the kind of work that currently takes your team hours, and that software can handle in minutes.

Hours → Minutes
Each system below replaces a manual process that currently takes your team significant time every week or month — with something that runs automatically in the background.
Built once. Runs forever.
We build each system into your operation and maintain it as part of your retainer. No one-off projects. No software licences. We own the outcome.
01
3-Way Purchase Order Matching
Automatically cross-references every supplier invoice against the original purchase order and the delivery note. Flags quantity shortfalls, price discrepancies, and duplicate billings before payment is authorised — so nothing gets paid that shouldn't.
Overpayments caught before they leave your account
02
Supplier Price Drift Detector
Compares every invoice line against your contracted unit rates. Suppliers who quietly increase prices above agreed rates are flagged instantly — not discovered 12 months later during a contract review, when recovering the money is far harder.
Price drift caught at the point of invoice
03
Supplier Statement Reconciliation
Supplier sends a PDF statement. The system reads it, maps every line to your purchase ledger, and outputs an exact reconciliation highlighting credits unclaimed, disputed invoices, and missing documents. A process that takes your AP team hours across all suppliers runs automatically in minutes.
Statement reconciliation — fully automated
04
Goods-In Discrepancy Auto-Logger
When a delivery arrives short or damaged, the warehouse photographs the delivery note. The system extracts quantities, compares them to the PO, and auto-generates a credit note request to the supplier within minutes — before the driver leaves the yard. Short deliveries that used to be absorbed as losses are now reclaimed.
Short delivery losses eliminated
05
Quote Normalisation Engine
Three suppliers quote in three different formats — different units, different payment terms, different lead times. The system reads every quote PDF and outputs a single normalised comparison table, with the cheapest option by line item highlighted and specification differences flagged. Procurement decisions become data-driven in seconds.
Better buying decisions on every PO
06
Job & Customer Profitability Analyser
Pulls actual labour hours, material costs, waste, rework, and delivery cost per job or customer — and calculates real margin. Many manufacturers find that a small number of customers or job types generate the bulk of their profit, while others quietly cost money. Knowing which is which changes every commercial conversation.
Real margin visibility — per job, per customer
07
Overtime & Agency Cost Monitor
Weekly automated report showing overtime hours and agency spend by department, shift, and production line — with comparison against budget and the previous 4 weeks. Ops managers get something specific to act on before the payroll bill lands, rather than seeing the damage after the fact.
Overtime visibility before the bill arrives
08
Slow-Moving Stock Early Warning
Flags any SKU that hasn't moved in 60+ days — before it becomes a write-off at year end. Shows the capital tied up, the storage cost accumulating, and recommended action (discount, return to supplier, or write off now). Prevents the year-end stocktake shock and frees working capital.
Write-offs prevented before they happen
09
Supplier Chaser Automation
Pulls every overdue purchase order and drafts a personalised chaser email calibrated to urgency — polite at 3 days, firmer at 10, escalated at 3 weeks. Sent automatically or approved with one click. What currently takes your procurement team hours every week becomes a 5-minute task.
Hours of weekly email chase-up → automated
10
Compliance Document Generator
Quality engineers fill in key facts — what failed, when, batch numbers, root cause. The system generates a fully formatted, ISO-compliant Non-Conformance Report in 90 seconds. Same for risk assessments and method statements when tendering for contracts. What takes 2–3 hours takes 2 minutes. Compliance doesn't slip because paperwork is too slow.
2–3 hrs per report → 2 minutes

Every system above is built into your retainer. We implement, run, and maintain them — you see the savings on your dashboard.
